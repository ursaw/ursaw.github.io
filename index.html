Bag with round corners

Cutting Pattern for bag with round corners Its quite simple to set up an cutting pattern for a bag as a cubus. You just need (2*(width+height)) x length and for the forehead side each width x height plus stitching allowance. Those sharp corners are not aesthetic at all and will be the first part of your bag to break. Therefore I developed a sheet to calculate a bag cutting pattern where you just input your dimensions of the bag and the (optional 2 different) roundings.
